Sudoku Unlimited

How to Play:

Sudoku is played on a grid of 9 x 9 spaces.

This grid contains 9 rows, coumns and boxes.

Fill out the entire grid using only the numbers 1 to 9, without repeating any numbers in each row, column or box.

  1. Press the New Game button to begin.
  2. Click on a grid square to select it, then click on a number to fill in that square.
  3. Fill in all the squares correctly to win.
